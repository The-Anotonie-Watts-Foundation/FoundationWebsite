About The Foundation

The Antonie Dwayne Watts Foundation, a community action group, was founded by Stacey Lee Moreau-Watts in 2023. She created this foundation in honor of her late husband Antonie Dwayne Watts who passed away October 11, 2023. Stacey and Antonie are both from Melville, Louisiana which also serves as a home base for the foundation. The Foundation looks to touch not only the lives of the Melville natives but pledge to make a difference in the lives of other’s who live in all of St. Landry Parish.



Foundation's Mission Statement

To help those in need regardless of race, religion, economic standing, or their background.
